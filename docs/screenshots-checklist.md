# Qwizzy Screenshots Checklist (App Store)

## iPhone (6.7" or 6.5")
1) Home / Lobby (CTA “Start a duel”)
2) Create Room (choose quiz, invite link)
3) In-game Question (timer + options)
4) Results / Scoreboard (winner highlight)
5) Daily Quiz (streak + progress)
6) Profile & Stats (badges, rank)

## iPad (optional)
- Home, In-game, Results

## Overlays (short text suggestions)
- “Duels en temps réel”
- “Invite tes amis en 1 lien”
- “Scores instantanés”
- “Défis quotidiens”
- “Progression & badges”

## Capture Tips
- Use real data (non-empty states).
- Keep overlays short (3–5 words).
- Avoid notification permission popups in screenshots.
- Use consistent device frame styling (or none).
